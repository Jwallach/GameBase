Aspects

Aspects are the traits of a game that go beyond things such as genre or simple tags. They often involve the philosophy of how a game is designed, or how they play out. For example, one RPG may feature a combat system where mashing the attack button will get you through the first half of the game no problem. Another might require you to counterplay enemy actions and create a game plan from the start of the game. Both fall under turn based combat and both are RPG's, but these games appeal to completely different players. Aspects help one distinguish between the genres in a more detailed and comprehensive way than traditional tags
